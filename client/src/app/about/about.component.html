about us

All of our products are hand-crafted overseas by minors in poor conditions, with the customer in mind. We strive to make the cheapest possible product, with the highest possible markup. All of our designs were completed in roughly 30 seconds by our top fashion designers. If you have any questions, we don't have a customer support team, so keep them to yourself.

This project is a webstore for clothes that implements java, Mean Stack, MongoDB, Express, and Angular. It has a home page, about page, store page, shopping cart page, checkout page, and order confirmation page. Our team name is CornCOB and our group members are Oliver Anderson, Cobey Szabo, Brock Goley.
